# SoulJahForLoveOS — GDPR & CCPA Compliance Statement

SoulJahForLoveOS is designed to be **fully compliant** with GDPR and CCPA because:

- it collects **no personal data**  
- it transmits **no data**  
- it stores everything **locally**  
- it has **no accounts, no analytics, no tracking**  

---

## 1. GDPR (EU)

Under GDPR, SoulJahForLoveOS is **not a data controller** because:

- no personal data is processed  
- no data is stored outside the user’s device  
- no data is transmitted to the developer  

Users retain full control of their data.

---

## 2. CCPA (California)

SoulJahForLoveOS is **not a “business” collecting personal information** under CCPA because:

- no personal information is collected  
- no data is sold or shared  
- no user profiles are created  

---

## 3. User Rights

Because SoulJahForLoveOS stores data **only on the user’s device**, all rights (access, deletion, correction) are handled directly by the user.
