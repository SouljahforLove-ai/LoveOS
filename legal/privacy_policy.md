# SoulJahForLoveOS — Privacy Policy

SoulJahForLoveOS is intentionally designed to protect your privacy by operating **entirely offline**.

---

## 1. Data Collection

SoulJahForLoveOS does **not**:

- collect personal data  
- transmit data to servers  
- store data outside your device  
- use cookies, analytics, or trackers  

All data remains on your machine under your control.

---

## 2. Local Storage

Any emotional states, identity maps, rituals, or system outputs are stored **locally** in files you control.

You may delete them at any time.

---

## 3. Third‑Party Sharing

SoulJahForLoveOS does **not** share, sell, or transmit data to any third party.

---

## 4. Children’s Privacy

The Software is not intended for children under 13.

---

## 5. Changes to This Policy

Updates may occur as the Software evolves.  
You are responsible for reviewing changes.
