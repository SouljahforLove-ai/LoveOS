# Emotional JSON Registry  
_The Structured Emotional Ontology and Machine‑Readable Emotional Definitions of LoveOS_

---

## 1. Purpose

The Emotional JSON Registry defines:

- every emotion  
- its family  
- its triggers  
- its needs  
- its action tendencies  
- its resolution logic  
- its dimensional vector  
- its sovereignty alignment  
- its fragmentation risk  
- its integration path  

This is the **database** of the emotional engine.

## 3. Core Emotional Families

- **Core Emotions**  
  Fear, Anger, Sadness, Joy, Disgust, Surprise

- **Social Emotions**  
  Shame, Guilt, Pride, Embarrassment

- **Identity Emotions**  
  Purpose, Meaning, Belonging, Alienation

- **Collapse Emotions**  
  Numbness, Overwhelm, Dissociation

- **Agape Emotions (Hidden)**  
  Compassion, Mercy, Grace, Steadfastness, Benevolence  
  *Operator‑only. Not public.*
