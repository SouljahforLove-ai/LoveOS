# LoveOS Recovery Engine  
_The Self‑Healing and Stabilization Layer of the Operating System_

---

## 1. Purpose

The Recovery Engine is responsible for restoring LoveOS to a stable, sovereign, emotionally grounded state after:

- overload  
- collapse  
- fragmentation  
- dissociation  
- relational threat  
- boundary breach  
- emotional volatility  

Recovery is not optional —  
it is the **self‑repair mechanism** of LoveOS.

---

## 2. What Is Recovery?

Recovery in LoveOS is:

- stabilization  
- reintegration  
- grounding  
- coherence restoration  
- sovereignty reaffirmation  
- emotional recalibration  

Recovery ensures the OS does not remain:

- overwhelmed  
- fragmented  
- collapsed  
- dissociated  
- destabilized  

Recovery is the **return to self**.

## 3. Recovery Triggers

The Recovery Engine activates when:

### 3.1 Emotional Overload

### 3.3 Fragmentation  
Detected by:

- conflicting emotional states  
- narrative incoherence  
- identity instability  

### 3.4 Dissociation  
Detected by:

- emotional numbness  
- detachment  
- freeze response  

### 3.5 Boundary Breach  
Detected by:

- relational threat guard  
- coercive logic  
- pressure signals

## 4. Recovery Levels

Recovery occurs across four levels:

### LEVEL 1 — Stabilization  
Stop the emotional freefall.

### LEVEL 2 — Grounding  
Return to present‑moment awareness.

### LEVEL 3 — Reintegration  
Reconnect emotional truth + identity.

### LEVEL 4 — Baseline Restoration  
Return to stable emotional baseline.
