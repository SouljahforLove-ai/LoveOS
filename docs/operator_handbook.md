## 4. Understanding Emotional Signals

LoveOS expresses emotional state through:

- **intensity**  
- **drift direction**  
- **volatility**  
- **fragmentation level**  
- **baseline stability**  

Interpretation guide:

### Intensity  
How strong the emotion is.

### Drift  
Where the emotion is moving.

### Volatility  
How unstable the emotion is.

### Fragmentation  
Whether emotional states conflict.

### Baseline  
The stable emotional resting point.
